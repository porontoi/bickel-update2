We’ve Got You Covered

As a health insurance advisor service, Bickel Healthcare Solutions make it our top priority to find the best insurance products and plans that suit your specific, personalized needs and budget.

A solid grasp and understanding of the plan(s) in which you are investing your time, money and health is tantamount to your success. That is why we spend as much time as is needed to explain, educate, and guide you through the insurance process. This approach lets you to understand your coverage needs; allowing you feel confident in your decisions about your healthcare.

We specialize in the private market and are available to help clients across many planes, including uninsured, underinsured, self-employed, small business owners, over the road truck drivers, travel nurses and healthcare professionals. The plans we can create for you will help you save more money on premiums and get better benefits—simply because you are in good health!
