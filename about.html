About Us

As the husband of an Emergency Room Nurse, I have a unique perspective on the importance of appropriate and comprehensive health insurance coverage; as well as the accompanying vision, dental and accident/disability policies that should supplement any health plan.

The wrong health coverage can lead to irreparable harm; in both the longevity of the policyholder and their families and the financial well-being of you and your family/dependents.


Most policies offered by employers and the Marketplace are overpriced and provide insufficient coverage. As a health insurance advisor, I make it my mission to educate potential clients on their options. The plans I offer will do what most cannot – reward your health status with health-based policies; and allow you to break free of the limits of an employer-based plan; which end when your employment ends.

If you feel as though your current plan is no longer cost-effective and you would like to explore a more tailored plan that better fits your budget; please book an appointment. It is my mission to empower you with the plan and tools that enhance your life and fall in line with your needs!

As my husband’s lovely assistant, I specialize in educating travel nurses and travel healthcare professionals on the freedom that a private, Nationwide PPO and supplemental vision, dental and accident/disability plan can bring to your travel career.

As a 15-year veteran nurse in the Emergency Department and current travel nurse, I have years of travel experience, both pre- and post- Pandemic.

As travelers, we always run the risk of contract cancellation, rate reduction and unexpected life changes that require us to go home to address. Additionally, we understand the fear of “what if I lose my coverage between contracts?”

My job is to facilitate a private plan the is not agency based AND does not end with your contract. A plan that offers continued coverage during our much-needed breaks and provides a sense of security in the event of an unexpected medical leave.
